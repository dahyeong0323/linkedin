# Raw Material Template

Use this template when the user has not provided enough concrete material.

```text
1. Where/when did this happen?
2. What exactly did you observe, hear, read, or experience?
3. What first seemed interesting, strange, uncomfortable, or important?
4. What question did it create?
5. What deeper structure, incentive, behavior, positioning, or context do you want to explore?
6. What critique, alternative, or practical implication should be included?
7. Are there any facts, names, or details that must not be mentioned?
```

Do not draft until there is at least one concrete scene, case, or observation.
