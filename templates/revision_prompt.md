# Revision Prompt

Use this when revising a draft toward the corpus.

```text
Revise the draft so it resembles the user's corpus more closely.

Do not make it cleaner, more concise, more polished, or more professional than the corpus.
Reduce generic LinkedIn cadence.
Remove viral hooks, punchy one-liners, excessive spacing, startup-newsletter voice, and abstract thesis openings.

Make the opening more concrete.
Strengthen the "그런데" turn.
Move from surface issue to structure.
Preserve a plain, slightly repetitive, reflective rhythm.
Keep the ending quiet and short.

Output only the revised Korean post.
```
